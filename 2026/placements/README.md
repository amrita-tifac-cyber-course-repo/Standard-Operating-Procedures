# Standard Operating Procedures

## Placement Coordinator

-	Act as the single point of contact between the department and CIR.
-	Coordinate department-specific placement and industry requirements.
-	Disseminate CIR communications to students and faculty.
-	Encourage participation in CIR-organized training and industry programs.
-	Support CIR during placement drives by ensuring student participation and availability
-	Ensure timely student registration for:
    -	Pre-placement talks
    -	Assessments
    -	Interviews
-	Share all CIR communications promptly with students.
-	Participate in review meetings organized by CIR.
-	Propose department-specific improvements.
-	Escalate issues related to:
    -	Student grievances
    -	Policy clarifications
-	Collect student feedback after placement and industry activities.
-	Ensure adherence to CIR placement policies, including:
    -	Offer acceptance rules
    -	Multiple-offer policies
    -	Withdrawal and eligibility norms
-	Collect and verify Offer letters
-	Maintain department-level records of placed students, company details and compensation packages
